Our Services Including But Not Limited To:

Student Visa

The Australian Student visa program is for international students who want to have full time study rights in Australia at all levels of education. Australian student visa provides the opportunity to the international students to study in high quality educational institutions in Australia.

Skilled Migration & Global Talent Visa

Skilled Migration Visa

General Skilled Migration Program is specifically designed to target highly professional and skilled people to migrate and work in Australia and contribute to the Australian economy. To be eligible to apply Skilled Visa the client needs to nominate an occupation and pass a point test with a minimum score of 65 points. There are different visa subclasses and options in point based skilled migration visa including visa sub class 189(Permanent) as an Independent Skilled Visa, subclasses 190(Permanent) and 491(Temporary) which you can be nominated by a state or territory government agency, or be sponsored by an eligible relative in Australia.

Global Talent Visa

This permanent visa has been designed for talented and highly skilled professional's applicants to migrate to Australia.

Business Visa

Australian Business & Investor visas are designed for people who are looking for the opportunity of investing or running a business in Australia. This visa will allow visa holder and are his/her immediate family to migrate to Australia and has a full-time work and study rights for all family members.

Employer Sponsored Visa

Employer Sponsored visa is designed to allow Australian businesses to fill employment shortages with overseas employee with required skill level. There are three different employer visa categories which are available for the clients who wish to work for an approved Australian sponsor if they can meet the eligibility requirements.

• Visa sub class 494 : Skilled Employer Sponsored Regional (Provisional) Visa
• Visa Subclass 482: Temporary Skill Shortage visa
• Visa Subclass 186: Permanent Visa

Partner & Family Visa

Partner Visa

Partner Visa can be applied by a partner of an Australian Citizen/ permanent resident or an eligible New Zealand citizen, who are either married or in a de facto relationship. With our Partner visa services we will check your eligibility and will help you with your documents preparation to support your application.

Family Visa

Australian Family visas have been designed to support applicants wishing to reunite immediate and extended family members. There are different categories of Family visas which we can check your eligibility and help you to apply for the most suitable scheme.

Tourist And Working/Work & Holiday Visa

Tourist Visa

If you are going to travel to Australia to visit a family/friend, for a holiday or to conduct a short business trip, you will need to apply for an Australian Visitor Visa. Depending on purpose and duration of travel, you can apply for different visitor visa stream. At Unikey Global, we will help you to apply for a right visitor visa category and support your application.

Working/Work & Holiday Visa

This visa is available for designated countries passport holders and has been designed for young people to travel to Australia and work while they are on holiday in Australia.
